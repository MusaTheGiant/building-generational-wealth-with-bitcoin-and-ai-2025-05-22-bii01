What is Affiliate Marketing? (And Why It's Perfect for 2025)

Affiliate marketing is a performance-based business model where you earn commissions by promoting other companies' products or services through unique tracking links. When someone makes a purchase through your link, you earn a percentage of the sale, often ranging from 5% to 100% depending on the program.

Essential Affiliate Marketing Concepts You Must Understand

Cookie duration determines how long you'll receive credit for a referral. A 30-day cookie means if someone clicks your link today and purchases within 30 days, you still earn the commission. This is crucial for maximizing your earning potential.

The key to affiliate marketing success isn't just driving traffic, it's about building trust and providing genuine value to your audience.

High-Ticket vs. Low-Ticket Affiliate Marketing
