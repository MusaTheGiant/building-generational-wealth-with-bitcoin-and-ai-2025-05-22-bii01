Breaking: Congress Passes Historic GENIUS Act

The United States Congress has made history by passing the GENIUS Act (Guiding and Establishing National Innovation for U.S. Stablecoin), the country's first major standalone cryptocurrency legislation. This landmark bill passed the House with overwhelming bipartisan support (308-122) and is heading to President Trump's desk for signature.
