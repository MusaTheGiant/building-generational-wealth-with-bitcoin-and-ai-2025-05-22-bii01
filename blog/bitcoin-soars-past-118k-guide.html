Dollar-Cost Averaging: The Proven Strategy

Instead of trying to time the market perfectly, spread purchases over time to reduce volatility impact.

üìà

DCA Example

Invest $500 monthly regardless of price. When Bitcoin dips, $500 buys more Bitcoin. When Bitcoin rises, $500 buys less Bitcoin. Over time, this averages to reasonable cost basis. Small difference between buying at $100K vs $120K when target is $200K.
